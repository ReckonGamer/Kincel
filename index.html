ABOUT US

Kincel is an application that delivers clothing, outfits and dresses rapidly to customers. Thanks to A.I. technology, Kincel adapts to your masses and advises you professionally to find the perfect outfit for your upcoming event and to order it within hours.

WHO ARE WE?

The founding team consists of Sonia Marques Cabo (law student), Severin Steiner (economics student), Noa Durante (laboratory assistant) and Nico Fehr (computer scientist).

OUR VISION

We want to revolutionize the delivery industry by making it possible for people to dress up according to their preferences in the shortest time. Also, we want to offer a sustainable way to help the clothing industry recycle used clothes.
